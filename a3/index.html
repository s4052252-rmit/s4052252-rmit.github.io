SONDER

/ˈsɒn.dər/ noun [ U ]

the strong feeling of realizing that every person you see has
their own life story in which they are the most important person.

A cloud of backstory and inside jokes

and characters strung together

with countless other stories you'll never be able to see.

In the small moments

-a tram stop, a chess move, a hand held…

we catch hints of whole lives moving beside our own

we make space for connection

By noticing the people we pass,

and soften the edges of a busy day

the city becomes a collection of

living stories

each one worthy of a second look.
